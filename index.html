Frequently asked

Before your clinic starts.

Straight answers about access, pricing and the safe rollout.

Is CapDent free?

CapDent starts free for one clinic with up to 300 registered patients, core clinic-management tools and 1 GB of cloud storage.

What happens when the clinic reaches 300 patients?

After the new pricing system is safely released, existing records, visits, treatments, payments and appointments will continue working. Only registration of an additional patient will require CapDent Cloud.

Can I purchase CapDent Cloud today?

Not yet. The paid plan will be enabled only after Google Play billing and server-side subscription verification are fully tested.

What will CapDent Intelligence add?

It is planned to include advanced business statistics, trends, clinic comparisons and up to three clinics managed under one owner account.

Is the information shown in the product preview real?

No. Names, counts and financial values in the website preview are fictional demonstration data.
