Frequently asked questions

Clear answers before your clinic starts.

Is CapDent really free for dental clinics?

Yes. Core clinic-management features are available without a feature subscription, and every clinic receives 1 GB of cloud storage.

Which clinic roles can use CapDent?

CapDent supports owners, doctors and receptionists. The clinic owner creates the workspace, and staff can join using an invite process.

What can a clinic manage inside CapDent?

Clinics can manage patient records, appointments, waiting flow, treatments, follow-ups, payments, pending dues, reports and clinical uploads.

What happens when the clinic reaches 1 GB?

New file uploads can pause until files are removed or the clinic upgrades its storage. Existing records and already uploaded files remain accessible.

Where can I install CapDent?

CapDent is available on Google Play for Android. An iPhone version is planned for a later release.
