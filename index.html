Frequently asked

Before your clinic starts.

Straight answers about access, pricing and storage.

Is CapDent really free?

Yes. All current clinic-management features are included free, along with 1 GB of cloud storage.

What happens after 1 GB?

The product remains feature-complete. The clinic can purchase additional storage once store-based subscriptions are introduced.

Can receptionists use it?

Yes. CapDent supports owners, doctors and receptionists with role-based access suited to clinic responsibilities.

Is the information shown in the product preview real?

No. Names, counts and financial values in the website preview are fictional demonstration data.
